Why This Training Matters

This workshop will guide you in teaching Coding and Robotics to your Grade 4 learners. We're focusing on building strong foundations that prepare learners for their journey toward Grade 7 Technology.

Grade 4 is where we spark curiosity and build confidence. Your learners will discover that they can be creators, not just consumers, of technology. You'll learn to guide them through:

  • Computational Thinking - Breaking problems into manageable steps
  • Basic Coding - Using Scratch to bring ideas to life
  • Simple Robotics - Understanding how technology responds to instructions
  • Digital Citizenship - Using technology responsibly and safely

"In Grade 4, we plant the seeds of technological confidence that will grow throughout their educational journey."

- WCED C&R Team
